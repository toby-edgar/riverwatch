About

RiverWatch is a Social Enterprise committed to the restoration and enhancement of Freshwater. We are engaged in providing communities and businesses with the resources and support needed to monitor entire catchments. Our aim is to become Zero Waste, Carbon Positive and to have a full circle product stewardship.
