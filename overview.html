Why RiverWatch?


RiverWatch is a Social Enterprise committed to the restoration and enhancement of Freshwater. As we grow we will provide more access to water quality information. Our aim is to become Zero Waste, Carbon Positive and to have a full circle product stewardship.


RiverWatch Software


RiverWatch software comes free with your Waka. The software opens up the power of your data by running algorithms which combine sensor parameters for whole systems interpretation of changes in water quality.

The data is available in easily understandable summaries and a water health score gives you water quality ‘at-a-glance’. users can access raw data sets, historic data and can publish graphs to quickly see trends in data.


our database architecture lets you receive alerts for sudden changes in water quality plus we have built in predictability and early warning systems for rapid and agile water quality management.
